# Contributing to OLAMIP

*Copyright © 2025 Ralph Gonzalez – https://olamip.org*

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the OLAMIP project.

At this time, **external contributions are not being accepted**.  
The protocol and its reference materials are currently in a controlled development phase to ensure stability, consistency, and alignment with the core design goals.

## Why Contributions Are Closed

The OLAMIP specification is intended to remain unified and canonical.  
During the early lifecycle of the protocol, changes must be carefully coordinated to avoid fragmentation or incompatible interpretations.

Once the specification reaches a stable maturity level, community input and collaboration may be opened in a structured way.

## What You *Can* Do

Even though contributions are closed, you are welcome to:

- Use the protocol freely, including in commercial projects  
- Build your own tools, libraries, or integrations  
- Report issues or questions via the issue tracker  
- Suggest ideas or improvements for future consideration  

## Future Plans

A formal contribution process will be introduced once the protocol reaches a stable release milestone.  
This will include guidelines for:

- Proposing enhancements  
- Submitting implementation improvements  
- Participating in discussions about future versions  

Thank you for your understanding and interest in the OLAMIP protocol.
